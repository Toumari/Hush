Privacy Policy

Hush - White Noise & Sound Mixer • Effective February 2026

Overview

Hush ("we", "the app") is a white noise and ambient sound mixer for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Hush does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or files.
  • We do not track your usage or create user profiles.

Locally Stored Preferences

The app stores your settings (such as your last sound mix and premium status) locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

The free version of Hush displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings under Settings > Google > Ads.

Premium users who purchase the ad-free upgrade will not see any advertisements, and no ad-related data collection will occur.

In-App Purchases

Hush offers a one-time premium upgrade processed through Google Play. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Hush is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Permissions

Hush requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — Required for loading advertisements and processing in-app purchases.
  • Foreground Service — Required to continue playing audio when the app is in the background.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — Required to display playback controls in the notification shade.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
